Bacara

Commander Bacara was one of, if not, the most effective Clone Commanders during the clone wars.

He was known as the most brutal Commander for good reason.

His platoon would lose many of its troops and were known for using hand to hand combat against battle droids.

The armor the clones wore were meant for harsh climates and many troopers died due to harsh environments.

Revan

Darth Revan was a Jedi in the old republic after the Mandalorian wars.

Because of his beliefs he was exiled from the order and his apprentice brought the Sith back.

During the Sith Jedi war Revan kills his apprentice and brings peace to the galaxy.

He is considered the first grey Jedi.
